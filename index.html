What am I looking at? ▼
Road Surface Quality
100m road sections scored from phone sensor data. Lower scores indicate rougher or less comfortable surface conditions. This is a general surface-quality layer, not a specific pothole detector.
Defect Signals
Localised impact clusters detected from repeated burst evidence. Redder signals indicate stronger impacts. More solid signals indicate higher confidence from repeat observations, recency, and supporting drive evidence.
